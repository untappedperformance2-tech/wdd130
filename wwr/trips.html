Featured Trips

Scenic Family Float

Difficulty: Beginner

Duration: 3 hours

Price: $49 per person

Perfect for families and first-timers! This gentle float down the lower canyon offers stunning scenery and calm waters. Our guides provide basic instruction and share interesting facts about the local wildlife and history.

  • No experience needed
  • Minimum age: 5 years
  • Includes light snacks and drinks
  • Professional guide included

Rapid Runner

Difficulty: Intermediate

Duration: Full Day (6 hours)

Price: $99 per person

Get your heart pumping on this exciting intermediate trip! Navigate through Class II and III rapids while enjoying the beautiful canyon scenery. Perfect for those with some rafting experience.

  • Previous rafting experience recommended
  • Minimum age: 12 years
  • Includes lunch and refreshments
  • All equipment provided

Extreme White Water Challenge

Difficulty: Advanced

Duration: 2 Days (includes camping)

Price: $299 per person

For experienced rafters seeking the ultimate adventure! Tackle Class IV and V rapids over two days of intense white water action. Includes overnight camping under the stars.

  • Must have previous rafting experience
  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • All meals and camping gear included
  • Safety kayaker accompanies trip

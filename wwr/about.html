Our History

Founded in 2005 by river enthusiast Mike Rivers, Whitewater Rafting Adventures started with just two rafts and a dream. What began as a small operation guiding friends down local rivers has grown into one of the most respected rafting companies in the region. Over the past 20 years, we've guided thousands of happy rafters through some of the most beautiful and exciting white water in the country.

Today, we operate with a fleet of 20 rafts and employ over 15 certified guides. We've expanded our offerings to include trips ranging from gentle family floats to multi-day expeditions for experienced rafters. Through it all, we've maintained our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and creating unforgettable experiences for our guests.
